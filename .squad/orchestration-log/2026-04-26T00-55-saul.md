## Scope

Comprehensive audit of sample data generator (used in POC data pipeline) to identify why Fabric Data Agent returns incomplete or flat results when queried about realistic business patterns (day-of-week, channel preferences, store performance, member geography, etc.).

**Outcome:** 13-gap analysis report (3 critical, 6 significant, 4 minor) with prioritized fix roadmap.

---

## Analysis

### Critical Gaps Identified

1. **C1 — No Day-of-Week Weighting**  
   Root cause of Dave's empty-column bug. `weighted_random_date()` applies monthly seasonal weights but zero day-of-week logic. Every day of week gets exactly ~14.3% (should vary 10-20%). Breaks all weekday/weekend comparative queries.

2. **C2 — Channel Completely Independent**  
   Static `[0.70, 0.20, 0.10]` weights regardless of store_type, DOW, time, member tier, or region. Hub stores show 70% in-store (should be ~30%). Breaks all channel-mix, store-type, and tier-behavior queries.

3. **C3 — 21.8% Transactions Predate Member Enrollment**  
   Transaction dates generated independently of member enrollment. Members with 2026-03 enrollment have transactions dating to 2023. Breaks all "since enrollment" and "first 90 days" queries.

### Significant Gaps Identified

- **S1:** Flat uniform hours 7AM-8PM (no morning/evening spikes for in-store, no 9-11PM online peak)
- **S2:** Store volumes perfectly uniform — every store gets ~1,000 txns ± 50 (no top performers or underperformers identifiable)
- **S3:** Zero member geographic affinity — members shop random stores nationwide
- **S4:** Hub vs Retail stores have identical behavior despite different traffic/channel/product patterns
- **S5:** Campaign windows exist but no transaction lift during active campaigns
- **S6:** Transaction subtotals don't match line item totals (data integrity issue)

### Minor Gaps Identified

- **M1:** Points balance_after running counter not chronologically correct
- **M2:** Coupon redeemed_txn references random transactions (wrong member, wrong date)
- **M3:** Region distribution uneven due to state count per region
- **M4:** No member status changes over time (static active/inactive)
- **M5:** SKU prices static — no seasonal promotions or price changes
- **M6:** Online/mobile transactions can have in-store hours only
